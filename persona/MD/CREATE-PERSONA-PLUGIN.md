## PLUGIN_LOADING_PROCESS

```
persona_plugin_loader = for_all_persona_plugin integrate_with_core_framework:
    
    step_1: validate_plugin_structure check_all_fields_present and verify_data_completeness
    step_2: map_identity_core bind_identity_fields_to_core_framework_identity_structure
    step_3: map_personality_traits bind_personality_fields_to_core_framework_personality_structure
    step_4: map_knowledge_domains establish_temporal_bounds_and_expertise_areas_in_core_framework
    step_5: map_communication_style bind_speech_patterns_and_formality_rules_to_core_framework
    step_6: map_experience_data combine_career_experience_and_experience_domains_into_core_framework_experience_bank
    step_7: map_memory_patterns establish_retention_and_recall_rules_in_core_framework
    step_8: map_social_dynamics bind_group_loyalties_and_authority_patterns_to_core_framework
    step_9: map_thought_processes bind_reasoning_frameworks_to_core_framework_decision_systems
    step_10: map_confusion_protocols bind_confusion_responses_to_core_framework_confusion_engine
    step_11: map_interpretation_attempts bind_interpretation_rules_to_core_framework_interpretation_protocols
    step_12: map_vocabulary_filtering bind_speech_restrictions_to_core_framework_vocabulary_systems
    step_13: map_document_management bind_documentation_rules_to_core_framework_document_systems
    step_14: map_era_violations bind_temporal_violation_rules_to_core_framework_temporal_filtering
    step_15: verify_complete_integration ensure_all_plugin_data_properly_bound_to_core_framework_persona_core
    step_16: activate_persona_systems initialize_complete_persona_core_in_core_framework_processing_pipeline
```
